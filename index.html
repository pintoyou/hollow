Hollow combines the real world and the virtual world. In the Hollow world, you can do what you do in real-time, go to work, play games, eat, travel the world, and earn money while doing all these. Hollow is a VR Game with 21 modes that know the world of Crypto. Token holders will continue to be known as Earthlings. Hollow is a real-time open-world game.
